Privacy Policy for SamX

Last updated: April 24, 2025

SamX (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that you don’t need to provide any personal information or grant any permissions in order to use our website or apps (including EtherX).

1. No Personal Data Collection

  • Zero Data Required: You can use all SamX services—study tracker, to‑do list, calculator, calendar, notes, NCERT books—without creating an account or providing any personal details.
  • No Permissions Needed: Our apps function entirely offline and do not request access to your device’s contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other resource.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. We have no visibility into how you use the app. All functionality works locally on your device or in your browser.

3. Local Data Storage Only

  • On‑Device Storage: Any notes, to‑do items, or study‑tracker entries you create are stored locally on your device (in your browser’s local storage or app sandbox).
  • You’re in Control: To clear your data, simply use your browser/app settings to clear local storage or uninstall the app.

4. Security & Updates

  • Security: Since we don’t collect or transmit any data, there’s nothing stored on our servers. All operations occur on your device.
  • Updates: We may push app updates or bug fixes, but these do not involve any data transfer beyond the app’s code itself.

5. Third‑Party Links

SamX may link to external resources (e.g., NCERT website). We have no control over those sites, and this policy does not apply to them.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy if our practices change. Any updates will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date. If we ever start collecting data, we’ll inform you clearly in-app and via this policy.
