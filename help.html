Troubleshooting Tips

  • PWA install didn’t show: Make sure you visit via HTTPS and check your browser’s install prompts.
  • Dark mode not sticking: Clear your cache or reload the page after toggling the theme.
  • Calculator buttons unresponsive: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings.

Accessibility Guide

  • Increase font size: Use your browser’s zoom controls (Ctrl + / Ctrl –) or adjust in Settings.
  • High-contrast mode: Toggle Dark Mode for a darker background and lighter text.
  • Screen reader: EtherX includes ARIA labels—ensure your screen reader is in focus mode to read buttons and inputs.

Clear App Data

If you’d like to reset EtherX and remove all your study history, subjects, and settings, follow these steps:

  1. Open your browser’s Settings menu.
  2. Go to Site Settings (or Privacy & Security).
  3. Find Storage or Local Storage for this site.
  4. Select Clear Data or Delete Site Data.
  5. Reload EtherX—your data will be reset and the app will start fresh.

Once cleared, you can re-add subjects, start new timers, and rebuild your history from zero.
