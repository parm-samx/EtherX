Troubleshooting Tips

  • Can’t install the app? Make sure you are on a secure (HTTPS) page. Look for “Install EtherX” in your browser menu.
  • Dark mode not staying on? Try refreshing the page after you switch themes.
  • Buttons not working? Check that you haven’t disabled scripts in your browser settings.
  • Data not saving? Make sure your browser allows storage for this site.

Use EtherX Offline

After you install EtherX, you can use it without internet. You install it once, and it works like a normal app on your device.

To install:

  1. Open EtherX in your browser.
  2. Tap the browser menu and pick “Install EtherX.”
  3. Accept to add it to your home screen or desktop.

Clear All Data

If you want to start fresh and erase all your history and settings, follow these steps:

  1. Open your browser’s settings.
  2. Find storage or data for this site.
  3. Choose to clear or delete that data.
  4. Reload EtherX—the app will be like new.

Warning: This can’t be undone.
