About SamX

Your Study Partner

SamX is a small, friendly company started by Parmeshwar. We make easy apps that help students and learners do their best. Our main app is EtherX. With EtherX, you can:

  • Track your study time: See how long you spend on each subject.
  • Plan your tasks: Make simple to-do lists and check off each item.
  • Save your notes: Keep all your class notes and ideas in one place.
  • Work offline: Even without internet, EtherX still works smoothly.

At SamX, we believe success comes from clear goals and steady work every day. When you use EtherX, you set goals, watch your progress, and feel proud of each achievement. Our app is built to be fast, simple, and helpful. You don’t need to be a computer expert—just tap, type, and go!

We care about making learning fun and easy. That’s why EtherX has bright buttons, clear labels, and simple screens. Whether you’re studying math, planning a project, or saving your notes, EtherX is your study partner.

Join us at SamX and try EtherX today. Let’s learn, grow, and succeed—one small step at a time!
